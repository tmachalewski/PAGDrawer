⚠️ Interpretation notes
  • Live vs Backend — Live includes UI/AC env VCs and active merge groups; Backend doesn't.
  • Structural artifacts — ATTACKER, COMPOUND (Initial State), and BRIDGE (INSIDE_NETWORK) are counted as nodes.
  • Duplication — 2-layer model duplicates CVEs across L1/L2. Chain depth produces `:d0`/`:d1` nodes. Granularity sliders multiply further.
  • Visibility toggles — Hiding a type removes its nodes/edges and adds bridge edges. Net edge count can go up or down.
  • Environment filtering (UI/AC) — Dims, does not remove. Filtered CVEs still appear in counts.
  • CAN_REACH — Mixes ATTACKER→HOST and INSIDE_NETWORK→L2-host edges.
  • HAS_STATE — Backend: 2 (AV:N, PR:N). Live: 4 (+UI, +AC env VCs).
  • Drawing quality metrics — Purchase (2002). Crossings excludes edge pairs sharing a node. Area uses center-point method in logical coordinates (zoom-invariant). Edge length CV uses population std dev.
